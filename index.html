Everything you need to know about CloudNova's services and solutions.

CloudNova is a multi-cloud solutions provider with deep expertise in Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and Google Cloud Platform (GCP). We also support hybrid and on-premises environments through our Hybrid Cloud services. Our architects are certified across all major platforms and will recommend the best fit for your business needs.

Migration timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple Lift-and-Shift migration for a small application can be completed in 2–4 weeks. A full enterprise re-architecture with multiple workloads typically takes 3–6 months. We begin with a thorough Discovery and Assessment phase to give you a precise timeline before any work begins.

Security is our top priority. We use AES-256 encryption for all data in transit and at rest, implement network segmentation and private connectivity, and follow a Zero Trust security model throughout. All migrations are audited, and we maintain compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO 27001 standards. We also conduct thorough penetration testing post-migration.
